Payment terms

Any project fee charged at £1000 or under will require the full fee invoice paid upfront for work to commence. Any project fee charged above this amount will be split into two 50% payments. The first 50% will be required for work to commence, and the second 50% will be required for handover.

Alternative payment terms will be discussed on projects charged at £10000 or above.

Website hosting

The website hosting service is charged at £10 / month and guarantees your website stays online and any issues that do come up are dealt with promptly. This service includes a free SSL/TLS certificate for securing your website.

Website maintenance

Maintenance is charged at £30 per every 20 minutes of work required to implement the requested updates to your website.
